# Parallel Universe App

A simple interactive web app that shows different possible versions of your future based on your choices.

---

## What it does

You enter:

- Your name  
- Your interests  
- Your current situation  
- A big decision you are thinking about  

The app generates 3 possible future paths showing how your life could unfold depending on different choices.

---

## Features

- Create multiple future timelines  
- Explore "what if" life scenarios  
- Click and view different life paths  
- Chat with your "future self" (optional feature)  
- Simple and interactive design  

---

## Purpose

This project helps users:

- Think about important decisions  
- Explore different life possibilities  
- Understand how choices can change the future  
- Have fun imagining alternative outcomes  

---

## Concept

Your choices create parallel futures. This app lets you explore them.
