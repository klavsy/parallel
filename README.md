🌌 **Parallel Universe**

Parallel Universe is an interactive decision simulation web application that helps users explore alternate versions of their future based on personal context, goals, and life choices.

By entering key details such as interests, current situation, and important decisions, users are immersed in dynamically generated “parallel life timelines.” Each timeline presents a unique possible future - highlighting career paths, life milestones, and long-term outcomes shaped by different decisions.

This transforms decision-making into an engaging, reflective, and imaginative experience - allowing users to explore the question:''

“What if I chose differently?”

### ✨ Key Features

### 🔮 AI-Powered Future Simulations
- Generates multiple alternative life timelines based on user input
- Creates unique future scenarios driven by different decisions
- Explores possible long-term consequences, opportunities, and life outcomes

### 💼 Career Path Discovery
- Suggests potential future job roles aligned with each timeline
- Visualizes career progression and professional milestones
- Highlights industries, opportunities, and possible future occupations

### 🍽️ Lifestyle & Food Recommendations
- Recommends places to eat based on the user's simulated lifestyle and location
- Suggests local cuisines, restaurants, and foods users may enjoy in each timeline
- Creates a richer and more realistic vision of daily life in alternate futures

### 🌍 Travel & Exploration
- Recommends destinations and places to visit throughout a user's simulated journey
- Suggests cities, landmarks, cultural experiences, and travel opportunities
- Helps users visualize how different life choices could influence where they live, work, and explore

### 📖 Interactive Storytelling
- Presents immersive narrative-based future journeys
- Highlights career progression, life events, travel experiences, and major milestones
- Encourages self-reflection through personalized storytelling
- Text-to-voice narration

### 🗺️ Location Intelligence
- Integrates **Bing Maps** to provide geographic context and visualization
- Helps users discover relevant places associated with their alternate future paths
- Uses map embedding without requiring a dedicated Maps API integration

### 📅 Future Planning
- Includes **Add to Outlook Calendar** functionality
- Allows users to save future goals, milestones, and important events directly to their calendar

### 🔗 Social Sharing
- Save / share image feature
- Integrated **LinkedIn Share** functionality
- Enables users to share generated timelines and insights with their professional network

### 📊 User Experience Analytics
- Powered by **Microsoft Clarity**
- Provides behavioral insights, session recordings, and heatmaps to improve usability and engagement
