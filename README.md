Parallel Universe App

A simple interactive web app that shows you different possible versions of your future based on your choices.

💡 What it does

You enter:

Your name
Your interests
Your current situation
A big decision you are thinking about

Then the app creates 3 possible future paths showing what your life could look like depending on different choices.

✨ Features
Create multiple future timelines
Explore “what if” life scenarios
Click and view different life paths
Chat with your “future self” (optional feature)
Simple and interactive design
🎯 Purpose

This project helps people:

Think about important decisions
Explore different life possibilities
Understand how choices can change the future
Have fun imagining “what could happen”
